Problem Statement:-

Write a program to calculate the sum of the digits of a positive integer.
For example, the sum of the digits of the number 123 is calculated as 1 + 2 + 3, which equals 6.

Your program should take a positive integer n as input and output the sum of its digits.

Input:

A positive integer n.

Output:

The sum of the digits of the input integer n.

Example:

Input: 123
Output: 6

Input: 456789
Output: 39
